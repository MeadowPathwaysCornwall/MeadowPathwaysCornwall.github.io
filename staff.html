Staff area

Internal resources and staff links. This area contains protected information and is for authorised staff only.

Access

Staff should not rely on client-side password protection. If you need access to internal materials, request access using the contact email below and we will provide secure delivery.

Request access to protected resources

To request access to internal documents, policies or the staff rota, please email a request to meadowpathwayscornwall@outlook.com from your staff email. Include your name, role and the resources you need.

Once verified, authorised staff will receive secure links or instructions for safe access. Client-side password forms are not a secure method for protecting sensitive documents and have been removed from the public site.
