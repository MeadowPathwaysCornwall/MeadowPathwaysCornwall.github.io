Meet the Team

Michelle Pascoe

Founding Director & Lead Therapist

Michelle has over 20 years’ experience as a qualified counsellor and play therapist. She specialises in trauma-informed, person-centred counselling and play therapy, supporting children and young people with SEN, SEMH, educational trauma, bereavement, and Emotionally Based School Non-Attendance (EBSNA). Michelle is also qualified in Senior Mental Health, SPACE, TIS, Emotion Coaching, and more.

Email: Michelle.Pascoe@meadowpathwayscornwall.com

Zoe Waitz

Therapist, Education Lead & Safeguarding Deputy

Zoe brings extensive experience in therapeutic education and wellbeing. She works closely with children and families to create safe, engaging, and meaningful learning opportunities. Her expertise includes SEMH support, therapeutic interventions, and guiding young people through transitions with care and consistency.

Email: Zoe.Waitz@meadowpathwayscornwall.com
