Meet the Team

Michelle Pascoe

Role: Founding Director & Lead Therapist

Michelle is a qualified counsellor and play therapist with over 20 years’ experience supporting children and young people with SEN, SEMH, trauma, bereavement, and EBSNA. She leads with warmth, creativity, and a deep commitment to emotional safety.

Zoe Waitz

Role: Founding Director & Education Lead

Zoe brings a wealth of experience in inclusive education, curriculum design, and relational practice. She works closely with families and professionals to ensure every learner’s journey is guided with care and purpose.
