Passwords & access notice

Safe request‑access process for staff pages and protected content

Why we use request‑access (not public passwords)

To keep protected information safe, staff pages use a request‑access flow rather than publishing passwords on public pages. This helps reduce the risk of unauthorised access and ensures we can verify who is requesting entry.

If you need access to staff resources, use the form below and we’ll respond promptly. For urgent needs, contact Michelle or Zoe directly.

Request staff access

Complete this form to request access to the staff area or protected resources. We’ll confirm your identity and provide the appropriate route.
