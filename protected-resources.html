About this area

This area stores staff-only resources such as supervision notes, safeguarding logs, sensitive templates and internal forms. These documents are not public and must only be accessed via secure, approved channels.

How to request access

  1. Complete the staff access request form on the Staff page with your full name, work email and reason for access.
  2. Requests are reviewed and approved by the director. Approved staff will receive secure instructions and time-limited links where appropriate.
  3. Do not share links or credentials; report any suspected breach immediately to the director.
Links shared externally will expire. If you need longer access for a specific task, request this when you submit your initial access form.

Downloadable secure links

For security, direct file links are only visible to authorised staff. If you have been approved, log in to the secure portal (instructions will be emailed) to access files.

Support

If you have questions about protected documents or need help with access, contact the director. For urgent safeguarding queries, use the DSL contact details on the Safeguarding page.
