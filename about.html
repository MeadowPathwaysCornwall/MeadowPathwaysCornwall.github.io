About Meadow Pathways

We provide trauma-informed, person-centred therapeutic and educational support for children and young people across Cornwall.

Our approach

Meadow Pathways offers tailored interventions that meet each child where they are. We combine play therapy, counselling, bespoke education planning and family support with a focus on emotional safety, trust and long-term wellbeing.

Person-centred practice

Therapies and educational work are guided by the child's strengths, preferences and pacing. We work collaboratively with families, schools and professionals to develop practical, trauma-aware plans that support re-engagement with learning and relationships.

Nature-inspired spaces

Our sessions often take place outdoors or in calm, welcoming spaces designed to reduce anxiety and encourage play, curiosity and connection.
