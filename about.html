Meet the Team - About Us

At Meadow Pathways Well Being and Education Cornwall, we are passionate about providing nurturing, personalised educations and support for children with SEMH and neurodiverse needs. Our leadership team brings together a wealth of expereince, specalist training, and a shared commitment to helping every child and guide individual journeys with care and purpose.

Zoe Waitz

Zoe is a qualified teacher with over 10 years experience with a background in special education and supporting students with finding their own paths with alternative provision. She has worked closely with children who struggle in mainstream settings, including those with anxiety, school avoidance (EBSNA) and complex behavioural needs, Neuro diverse or trauma needs. Zoe brings calm consistency, creative planning and deep understanding to her role - helping children rebuild confidence, re-engage with learning, and achieve positive outcomes. Zoe has a wealth of experience with Forest school which supports beyond practical skills, forest school fosters teamwork, problem-solving, resilience, and emotional regulation.
