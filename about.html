About Us

At Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall (MPWEC), we are passionate about providing nurturing, personalised education and support for children with SEMH and neurodiverse needs. Our leadership team brings together a wealth of experience, specialist training, and a shared commitment to helping every child — guiding individual journeys with care and purpose.

Meet the Team

Zoe Waitz

Director of Education. Zoe is a qualified teacher with over 10 years’ experience working in special education. She supports children and young people with anxiety, school avoidance (EBSNA), neurodiversity, and trauma-related needs. Zoe’s child-centred approach helps learners grow in confidence and re-engage with learning. She’s passionate about nature-based education and can often be found in the veg patch or exploring Cornwall’s wild corners with her family.

Michelle Pascoe

Director of Therapies. Michelle has worked in SEN settings for over 20 years. She is a person-centred counsellor, play therapist, and senior mental health worker, trained in PACE, SPACE, and emotion coaching. Michelle supports children with SEMH, neurodiversity, and trauma-related experiences. Her safeguarding work is rooted in empathy and early intervention. Outside of work, she enjoys coastal walks with her dogs and time with her family.
