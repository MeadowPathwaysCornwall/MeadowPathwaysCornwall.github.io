About Us

Meadow Pathways Cornwall is led by a passionate team of professionals committed to nurturing, inclusive, and trauma-informed education. We believe every child deserves a safe and supportive environment where they can thrive emotionally, socially, and academically.

Zoe Waitz

Zoe is a founding director of Meadow Pathways Cornwall with a background in therapeutic education and child development. Her work focuses on creating safe, relational spaces for children with SEMH and neurodiverse needs. Zoe leads on curriculum design, therapeutic integration, and multi-agency collaboration.

Michelle Pascoe

Michelle is a founding director and leads on therapeutic provision, web development, branding, and organizational communications. She ensures the site reflects the ethos of nurturing, inclusivity, and professionalism. Michelle brings a blend of creative and technical skills to support the wellbeing and growth of every learner.

Our Ethos

We are committed to trauma-informed practice, relational safety, and flexible learning pathways. Our provision is shaped by the needs and strengths of each child, with a focus on building confidence, connection, and readiness for life beyond education.

Our Approach

Meadow Pathways Cornwall works closely with families, professionals, and local authorities to co-create bespoke packages of support. We offer a blend of therapeutic input, accredited learning, and real-world skills — all delivered in nurturing environments that promote regulation, resilience, and growth.
