About Meadow Pathways

Meadow Pathways provides inclusive therapeutic education and wellbeing support for children and young people with SEND and SEMH needs. We combine tailored learning, therapeutic practice, and community-based activities to build confidence, skills, and a sense of belonging.

Our Ethos

We work relationally, using trauma-informed practice and creative, curriculum-linked activities. Our work is guided by Safety, Playfulness, Acceptance, Curiosity, and Empathy — the SPACE ethos.

Our Values

  • Resilience & Independence – Helping children build confidence and autonomy through consistent support.
  • Health & Wellbeing – Prioritising emotional safety and therapeutic care in every setting.
  • Honesty & Integrity – Working transparently with families, schools, and professionals.
  • Friendship & Kindness – Creating safe, nurturing environments where children feel seen and valued.

Our Team

Led by Michelle Pascoe, a qualified counsellor and play therapist with over 20 years’ experience, our team brings warmth, creativity, and deep relational expertise to every child’s journey. We work collaboratively with families, schools, and professionals to ensure every learner receives the support they need.
