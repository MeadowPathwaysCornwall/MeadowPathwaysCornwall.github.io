About Meadow Pathways

Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall provides compassionate, trauma-informed support for children and young people. We focus on emotional safety, relationship-building, and practical learning to help each child thrive.

Our Approach

We use a person-centred, strength-based model drawing on therapeutic play, trauma-informed counselling, outdoor learning, and flexible education provision. Sessions are tailored, paced, and co-produced with families, schools, and agencies.

Leadership and Team

Michelle Pascoe — Founding Director and Lead Therapist. Qualified counsellor and play therapist with over 20 years’ experience supporting children and families.

Zoe Waitz — Partnership and operational lead, supporting referrals and service delivery.

Ethos and Values

  • Emotional Safety and Trust
  • Collaborative, Strengths-Based Practice
  • Inclusivity and Respect for Difference
  • Practical Skills and Resilience Building
