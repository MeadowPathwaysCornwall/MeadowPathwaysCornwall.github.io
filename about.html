About Meadow Pathways

Meadow Pathways provides trauma-informed, person-centred education and wellbeing support for children and young people across Cornwall. We specialise in supporting those with SEND, SEMH, Emotionally Based School Non-Attendance, bereavement, and the effects of trauma on learning and emotional regulation.

Our Approach
