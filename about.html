Who we are

Meadow Pathways provides trauma-informed, person-centred education and wellbeing support for children and young people across Cornwall. We specialise in supporting those with SEND, SEMH, Emotionally Based School Non-Attendance, bereavement, and the effects of educational trauma. Our ethos is rooted in safety, trust, creativity, and belonging.
