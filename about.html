About Michelle & Zoe

Michelle Pascoe

Michelle leads the therapeutic provision at Meadow Pathways, delivering therapy services and overseeing land-based tutoring programmes. She supports the wellbeing of all students and works closely with the Headteacher to integrate therapeutic and educational approaches.

Michelle is a dedicated practitioner with a background in community youth work and creative therapy. She ensures every child’s voice is heard and valued. With around 20 years of experience working with neurodiverse children, Michelle has developed a deep understanding of their needs, continuously enhancing her skills through training and qualifications in therapeutic practice.

Zoe Waitz

Zoe brings years of experience in education and Forest School/outdoor education interventions. She is passionate about helping young people reach their potential and is committed to safeguarding and inclusive practice.

Zoe has always specialised in working with neurodiverse children and supporting them to re-engage with education. As the overall lead of Meadow Pathways, Zoe is responsible for educational standards, safeguarding, compliance, and staff supervision. She ensures the delivery of high-quality, personalised education tailored to meet the diverse needs of every learner.
