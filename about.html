About Meadow Pathways

Meadow Pathways offers trauma‑informed, person‑centred therapeutic education for children and young people across Cornwall. We combine play therapy, counselling, Outdoor learning, and flexible curriculum delivery so learners can rebuild trust, emotional regulation and engagement with learning.

Our values

Resilience & Independence Health & Wellbeing Honesty & Integrity Friendship & Kindness

We prioritise safety, relationship-building, and slow, consistent support that honours each child’s story and pace of change.

Therapeutic education

We blend therapeutic approaches and curriculum aims so that emotional needs are supported alongside learning goals. Sessions take place in small, trusting environments — outdoors, community settings, and tailored spaces that reduce anxiety and support regulation.
