About Meadow Pathways Cornwall

Meadow Pathways Cornwall provides specialist education and well-being support tailored to children and young people who may not thrive in mainstream settings. We focus on trauma-informed care, inclusion, and holistic development.

Our dedicated team has extensive experience in education, mental health, and social care, ensuring that every learner’s unique needs are met with compassion and professionalism.
