About Meadow Pathways

Our mission

Meadow Pathways provides trauma-informed education and wellbeing support, championing every child’s right to feel safe and flourish. We build bespoke packages, blending therapeutic practice with practical learning.

Our values

We work from a relationship-first stance, combining empathy, consistency and creativity. Our SPACE approach supports emotional regulation, play and curiosity to help children re-engage with learning.

What we offer

  • Individual and small-group EOTAS packages
  • Play therapy and counselling
  • Outdoor learning and community-based sessions
