About Meadow Pathways Cornwall

Meadow Pathways Cornwall is dedicated to providing personalized and holistic education and well-being services for children and young people with unique needs across Cornwall.

Meet Our Team

Michelle Pascoe

Therapist & Safeguarding and Mental Health Team Lead

Michelle is a highly experienced therapist with a deep passion for supporting children and young people. With years of experience in safeguarding and well-being, Michelle leads the Meadow Pathways Cornwall team in ensuring every individual feels safe and valued. Her dedication to creating a nurturing and inclusive environment is at the heart of everything we do. Through her role, Michelle provides therapeutic support and guidance, helping children and young people thrive academically, emotionally, and socially.

Zoe Waitz

Teacher and Forest School Lead

Zoe is an innovative and skilled teacher with a strong commitment to outdoor education. As the Forest School Lead, Zoe combines her love for teaching with her passion for nature, creating unique learning experiences for children and young people. With years of experience in both traditional and alternative education settings, Zoe brings creativity, patience, and understanding to her role. Her work focuses on helping children build confidence, resilience, and a love for learning in a safe and supportive environment.
