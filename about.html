About Meadow Pathways

Meadow Pathways provides specialist, trauma-informed education and wellbeing support across Cornwall. Our approach is person-centred — we build trusting relationships and tailor learning to the needs of each young person.

Our approach

We use outdoor activity, creative practice and therapeutic teaching to develop resilience, practical skills and emotional regulation. Our programmes are delivered by skilled staff with a focus on safety, inclusion and positive outcomes.

Our team

Our staff team includes experienced teachers, wellbeing practitioners and support workers. For staff access and internal resources, please sign in on the Staff page.
