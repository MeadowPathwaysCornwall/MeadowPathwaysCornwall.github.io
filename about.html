About Meadow Pathways

Guiding Individual Journeys with Care and Purpose

Who we are

We provide trauma-informed, person-centred education and wellbeing support for children and young people across Cornwall. We specialise in supporting SEND, SEMH, EBSNA, bereavement, and the effects of educational trauma. Our ethos is rooted in safety, trust, creativity, and belonging.

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to thrive. Our holistic approach blends therapeutic practice with flexible, creative education that meets learners where they are — emotionally, socially, and academically.

What we offer

  • Mentoring & Advocacy — Consistent relational support.
  • Tailored Learning Packages — Flexible academic pathways.
  • Life Skills & Independence — Functional skills & community engagement.
  • Diagnostic Assessments — Literacy/numeracy profiling.
  • SEMH Interventions — Trauma-informed strategies.
  • Wellbeing & PSHE — Creative therapeutic sessions.
  • Transitional Services — Reintegration & onward pathways.
  • Therapeutic & Curriculum Activities — Nature-based activities linked to ASDAN/AQA.

Our approach

We build trusting relationships through warmth, empathy, and consistency, guided by Safety, Playfulness, Acceptance, Curiosity, and Empathy. We adapt our approach to each child’s needs using play therapy, creative activities, and community-based learning to help children re-engage, feel safe, and grow in confidence.
