About Meadow Pathways

Our approach

Meadow Pathways offers trauma-informed, relational support that helps children and young people re-engage with learning. Our approach is person-centred and tailored, combining therapeutic practice with practical learning opportunities in calm, nurturing environments.

We work closely with families and schools to create predictable, safe pathways back into education or alternative routes that support wellbeing and growth.

Our values

  • Safety and trust
  • Relationship-led practice
  • Collaboration and clarity
  • Practical, hands-on learning
