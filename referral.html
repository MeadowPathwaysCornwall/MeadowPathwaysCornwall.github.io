Referral guidance

Before submitting, please ensure you have parental consent (where required), recent school reports, any EHCP extracts, and a brief summary of presenting needs and risk. If urgent, contact the team directly by phone.

Referral form

Contact for help

If you prefer to email documents, use the contact page. Sensitive documents should only be shared via secure channels.

What happens next

  1. We acknowledge receipt and contact the referrer within 3 working days.
  2. We may arrange a phone consultation with family and referrer to gather further details.
  3. We agree a recommended pathway — EOTAS placement, therapeutic input or signposting — and plan next steps.
