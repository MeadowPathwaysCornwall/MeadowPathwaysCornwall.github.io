Referral Form

If you would like to refer a child or young person to Meadow Pathways, please complete the form below. Referrals can be made by families, schools, or professionals. Include as much relevant information as possible to help us assess needs and plan a safe, paced package of support.

Before you start

  • Please obtain parental consent before submitting personal information unless you are making a safeguarding referral.
  • Include relevant medical, safeguarding or SEND information to help us plan appropriately.
  • All referrals are screened and an initial response will be provided within 10 working days where possible.
