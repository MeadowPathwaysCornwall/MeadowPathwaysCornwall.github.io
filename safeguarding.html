Safeguarding at Meadow Pathways

At Meadow Pathways Wellbeing & Education Cornwall, safeguarding is our top priority. We are committed to creating a safe, nurturing environment for all children and young people, ensuring their wellbeing is central to everything we do.

Our Approach

Our approach blends trauma-informed practice, relational support, and clear safeguarding policies to protect children, staff, and partners. All staff receive regular safeguarding training and understand their responsibilities under statutory guidance.
