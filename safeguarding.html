Safeguarding and Policies

Safeguarding

Keeping children safe is our highest priority. Our staff are trained in safeguarding and child protection procedures, and we work closely with statutory partners to ensure the welfare of children and young people in our care.

View full policies

Reporting concerns

If you have a safeguarding concern, please follow your local authority guidance and contact the relevant safeguarding team. If the matter is urgent or a child is at immediate risk, call emergency services.
