Safeguarding at Meadow Pathways Cornwall

At Meadow Pathways Cornwall, safeguarding the welfare of children and young people is our top priority. We are committed to providing a safe, nurturing environment where every individual feels valued and protected.

Our safeguarding policies are designed to ensure that all staff, visitors, and volunteers follow best practices and adhere to statutory guidelines. Through ongoing training and robust procedures, we maintain the highest standards of child protection and support.
