Our Commitment to Safeguarding

At Meadow Pathways Cornwall, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to ensuring the safety, well-being, and protection of all children and young people we work with. Our goal is to create an environment where every individual feels safe, valued, and supported.
