Safeguarding and Child Protection

Meadow Pathways places the safety of children and young people at the centre of our work. We follow statutory guidance and best practice to protect children from harm and to promote their welfare. All staff are trained in safeguarding, and we maintain clear procedures for reporting concerns.

Key information

Please contact our Designated Safeguarding Lead if you have concerns. External agencies and local safeguarding partners will be involved as required to ensure safety.

Policies and procedures

Policy documents are available to staff and partners in the Staff Area. For confidentiality and security, our full policy documents are stored there and can be downloaded by authorised staff.
