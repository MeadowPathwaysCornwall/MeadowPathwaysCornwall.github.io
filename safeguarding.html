Safeguarding Our Commitment

At Meadow Pathways Cornwall, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to creating a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment for all children and young people in our care. Our approach is grounded in trauma-informed practices, ensuring that the physical, emotional, and mental well-being of every individual is our top priority.

We work closely with families, schools, and local authorities to develop comprehensive safeguarding policies and procedures that meet the highest standards. Our team is committed to continuous professional development, ensuring that we stay at the forefront of safeguarding practices and legislation.

If you have any concerns or questions about safeguarding at Meadow Pathways Cornwall, please do not hesitate to contact us. Together, we can create a nurturing and secure environment where every child can thrive.
