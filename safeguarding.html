Safeguarding at Meadow Pathways

At Meadow Pathways, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. Every child and young person has the right to feel safe, valued, and supported. Our team is trained in Safeguarding, Trauma Informed and follows clear policies to ensure the wellbeing of all learners. We work closely with families, local authorities, and safeguarding partners to create a culture of trust, openness, and protection.
