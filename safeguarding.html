Our Safeguarding Commitment

We ensure the safety, well-being, and protection of all children and young people we work with.

At Meadow Pathways Cornwall, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. We strive to create a safe, inclusive, and nurturing environment where every child feels valued and protected.

Our safeguarding policies are regularly reviewed and updated to meet the highest standards. All staff are trained to respond promptly and effectively to any concerns, working closely with families and local authorities to ensure the best outcomes for every child.
