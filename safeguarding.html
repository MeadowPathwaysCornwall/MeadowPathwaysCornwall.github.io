Safeguarding

At Meadow Pathways Cornwall, safeguarding is our top priority. We are committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for all our students, staff, and visitors. Our safeguarding policies are robust and regularly reviewed to reflect best practices and statutory guidance. If you have any concerns about the wellbeing or safety of a young person, please contact our designated safeguarding lead immediately.
