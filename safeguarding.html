Safeguarding and Child Protection

Safeguarding is central to Meadow Pathways. It means protecting children and young people from harm, promoting their welfare, and ensuring they grow up in safe, supportive environments. It includes preventing abuse, recognising signs early, responding promptly and appropriately, and working together with families and statutory agencies.
