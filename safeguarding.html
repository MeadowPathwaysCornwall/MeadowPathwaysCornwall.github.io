Safeguarding

Protecting children and young people is everyone’s responsibility

Our commitment

We uphold robust safeguarding practices across all settings. All staff are vetted and trained; we follow local authority guidance and statutory requirements, and we act promptly on concerns. Emotional safety is foundational to our SPACE ethos and embedded in every interaction.

  • Safer recruitment and enhanced DBS checks
  • Ongoing safeguarding training for all staff
  • Clear reporting pathways and designated safeguarding leads
  • Risk assessments for activities and environments

How to raise a concern

If you have a safeguarding concern, contact us directly. In an emergency, always contact emergency services.

Email: Michelle.Pascoe@meadowpathwayscornwall.com

We will respond and take appropriate action in line with our safeguarding policy.
