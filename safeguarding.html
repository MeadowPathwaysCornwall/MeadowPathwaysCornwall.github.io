Our Commitment to Safeguarding

At Meadow Pathways, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to creating emotionally safe, nurturing environments where children feel seen, heard, and protected. Our team follows statutory guidance and best practice, including Keeping Children Safe in Education (KCSIE), and we work closely with families, schools, and professionals to ensure joined-up support.
