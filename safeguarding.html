Safeguarding and Child Protection

Safeguarding is our highest priority. This page summarises key contacts, procedures and how to report concerns.

Reporting concerns

If you have immediate safeguarding concerns, contact the appropriate emergency services or local children’s social care. For non-urgent concerns, use the contact form and mark the message as safeguarding.

Designated Safeguarding Lead

Michelle — DSL and lead for safeguarding policy and practice. Senior leads will respond to safeguarding concerns and guide next steps.

Key documents

Important documents (KCSIE, safeguarding policy, recording templates) are stored in the staff area for verified staff. Public summaries are available where appropriate.
