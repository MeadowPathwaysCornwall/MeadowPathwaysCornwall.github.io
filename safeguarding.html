Safeguarding at Meadow Pathways

We are committed to creating a safe, nurturing environment for every child and young person. Our safeguarding approach is rooted in empathy, early intervention, and collaborative support with families and professionals. All staff are trained in safeguarding procedures and work proactively to identify and respond to concerns.

Our Approach

We follow national safeguarding guidelines and embed trauma-informed practices throughout our provision. Our team includes experienced safeguarding leads who work closely with children, families, and external agencies to ensure every child feels safe, heard, and supported.

Training & Support

Staff receive regular training in safeguarding, child protection, and emotional wellbeing. We also offer guidance and support to families navigating complex challenges, ensuring that safeguarding is a shared responsibility built on trust and transparency.
