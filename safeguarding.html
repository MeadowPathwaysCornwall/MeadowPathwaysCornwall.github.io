Safeguarding and child protection

Protecting children and young people with clarity, care and professional responsibility.

Reporting concerns

If you are concerned a child is being harmed, contact statutory services immediately. For Meadow Pathways practice concerns, contact our lead safeguarding staff using the details below.

Safeguarding at Meadow Pathways

Safeguarding is central to Meadow Pathways. We work to prevent harm, recognise signs of concern, respond promptly, and work with families and statutory agencies to keep children safe.

Why safeguarding matters

Every child has the right to feel safe and to be protected from neglect, abuse and exploitation. Effective safeguarding supports children to thrive and builds trusting professional relationships.
