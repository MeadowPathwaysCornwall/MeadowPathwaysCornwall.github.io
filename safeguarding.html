    • Safeguarding at Meadow Pathways

    Safeguarding is central to everything we do at Meadow Pathways. We believe that every child deserves to feel safe, respected, and emotionally supported. Our team is fully trained in Safeguarding, trauma‑informed practice and works closely with families and professionals to ensure the wellbeing of every learner.

    Michelle Pascoe is our Designated Safeguarding Lead (DSL), bringing over 20 years’ experience in therapeutic and educational support. Zoe Waitz is our Deputy Designated Safeguarding Lead (DDSL), with a background in pastoral leadership and inclusive education. Together, they ensure that safeguarding is embedded in every aspect of our provision.
