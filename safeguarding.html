Safeguarding

At Meadow Pathways Well-being and Education, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. We are fully committed to creating and maintaining a safe, supportive, and nurturing environment for all children, young people, staff, and visitors in the wider community.

We work with children of all ages, including those with neurodiverse needs, trauma-related experiences, EBSNA (Emotionally Based School Non-Attendance), bereavement, and those with Education Health and Care Plans.
