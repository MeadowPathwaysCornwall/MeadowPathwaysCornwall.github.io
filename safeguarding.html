Our Safeguarding Ethos

At Meadow Pathways, safeguarding is not a checklist — it is a culture of care. We believe every child and young person deserves to feel safe, valued, and protected, not just physically but emotionally and relationally.

Our practice is trauma-informed and guided by the SPACE ethos:

  • Safety – creating environments where children feel secure
  • Presence – being consistently available and reliable
  • Attunement – noticing and responding to children’s needs
  • Connection – building trust through authentic relationships
  • Empowerment – supporting children to find their voice and agency
