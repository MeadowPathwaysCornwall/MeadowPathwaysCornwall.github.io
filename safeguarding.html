Safeguarding

At Meadow Pathways Well-being and Education, the safety, welfare, and well-being of every child and young person is our highest priority.

We are committed to creating a nurturing and secure environment where children feel safe, respected, and listened to. We recognise our moral and statutory responsibility to safeguard and promote the welfare of all children, and we take this duty seriously.

Our provision is underpinned by robust safeguarding policies and procedures, in line with national guidance and local protocols. All staff are fully trained in safeguarding, child protection, and safer working practices, and we work in close partnership with families, local authorities, and other professionals to ensure the best possible outcomes for the young people in our care.
