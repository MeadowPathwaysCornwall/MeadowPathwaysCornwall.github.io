Our Safeguarding Commitment

At Meadow Pathways, safeguarding is woven into everything we do. We believe that every child and young person deserves to feel safe, valued, and protected — not just physically, but emotionally and relationally. Our trauma-informed approach ensures that safeguarding is not a checklist, but a culture of care.

We work with children and families navigating complex emotional landscapes, including SEMH, neurodivergence, EBSNA, and trauma. Our safeguarding practice is guided by the SPACE ethos — Safety, Presence, Attunement, Connection, and Empowerment — and shaped by the lived realities of those we support.
