Our Safeguarding Commitment

At Meadow Pathways Cornwall, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to ensuring the safety, well-being, and protection of all children and young people we work with. Our goal is to create a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment where every individual feels valued and supported.

Our safeguarding policies are regularly reviewed and updated to meet the highest standards. All staff are trained to recognise and respond promptly to any concerns, working collaboratively with families, schools, and local authorities to ensure the best outcomes for every child.

We strive to empower children and young people by building trust and promoting resilience, ensuring their voices are heard and respected. Together, we can build a brighter, safer future for every child.

Our Commitment to Safeguarding and Health & Safety

At Meadow Pathways, we take all aspects of safeguarding and health & safety extremely seriously. This includes the welfare and protection of the children and young people we work with, as well as our responsibilities around staff conduct, safe practice, and compliance with relevant legislation.

We adhere fully to up-to-date statutory guidance, including Keeping Children Safe in Education and Working Together to Safeguard Children. All members of staff receive regular safeguarding training and follow robust policies and procedures to ensure a safe and supportive environment.
