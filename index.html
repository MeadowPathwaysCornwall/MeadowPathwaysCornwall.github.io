Welcome

At Meadow Pathways Cornwall, we believe every child deserves to feel safe, seen, and supported. Our trauma-informed, relational approach meets children and young people where they are — whether through play, nature, or creative learning. We work alongside families and professionals to build trust, resilience, and a renewed sense of possibility.
