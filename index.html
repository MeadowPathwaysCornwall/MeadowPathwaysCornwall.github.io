Welcome to Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall

At Meadow Pathways, we walk alongside children, young people, and families across Cornwall, offering safe, nurturing spaces where every individual feels seen, heard, and supported.

Our work is rooted in a trauma‑informed, person‑centred approach. We specialise in supporting children and young people with Social, Emotional and Mental Health (SEMH) needs, Special Educational Needs (SEN), Emotionally Based School Non‑Attendance (EBSNA), bereavement, and the impact of educational trauma.

We believe in the power of SPACE — Safety, Playfulness, Acceptance, Curiosity, and Empathy. These values guide everything we do, from play therapy and emotion coaching to creative, flexible learning opportunities in the community.

Whether it’s through play, nature, creativity, or gentle re‑engagement with learning, our aim is to help children rebuild trust, confidence, and joy in themselves and the world around them.

We work closely with families, schools, and professionals to ensure that support is collaborative, consistent, and tailored to each child’s unique journey.
