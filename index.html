Welcome to Meadow Pathways

Therapeutic education and wellbeing support across Cornwall

Relational, creative, and rooted in emotional safety

Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall offers bespoke therapeutic education for children and young people who are unable to attend school due to emotional needs, trauma, SEN, or other barriers. We meet each child where they are, building trust, safety, and connection through flexible, person-centred support.

Our work is guided by the SPACE ethos — Safety, Presence, Attunement, Connection, and Empowerment — and shaped by the unique needs of every learner and family. Whether we’re working on the beach, in the woods, or rebuilding school skills, we create nurturing environments where children can thrive.

We collaborate closely with families, schools, and professionals to ensure joined-up support and meaningful progress. Every journey is different — and we walk alongside each child with care, creativity, and compassion.
