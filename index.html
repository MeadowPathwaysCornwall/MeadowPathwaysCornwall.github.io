What we offer

EOTAS Provision

Our EOTAS provision blends therapeutic practice with practical learning. We support literacy and numeracy, life skills and emotional regulation through small group and 1:1 work in nurturing outdoor and indoor settings.

We tailor individual learning plans and work closely with families, schools and multi-agency partners to create safe, predictable learning journeys.

Therapeutic Support

Play therapy and person-centred counselling are available for children and young people to process events, build resilience and re-engage with learning. Our team uses trauma-informed techniques to promote safety and trust.

Practical Activities

We use practical, hands-on activities—gardening, cooking, den-building, creative arts and multimedia—to develop confidence, social skills and independence.
