Welcome

Meadow Pathways provides inclusive therapeutic education and wellbeing support for children and young people with SEND and SEMH needs. We combine tailored learning, therapeutic practice, and community-based activities to build confidence, skills, and a sense of belonging.

Our approach

We work relationally, using trauma-informed practice and creative, curriculum-linked activities. Our work is guided by Safety, Playfulness, Acceptance, Curiosity, and Empathy.

  • Bespoke learning: Tailored packages, ASDAN, AQA Unit Awards and Functional Skills for curriculum subjects.
  • Therapeutic interventions: Play therapy, emotion coaching and SEMH interventions.
  • Community activities: Nature-based sessions, walking, bush craft and practical life skills.

What we offer

We provide a fully bespoke package of care and education that includes:

Mentoring and Advocacy

Building trust and self-belief through consistent relational support and Alternative Provisions.

Tailored Learning Packages

Flexible academic pathways matched to each learner’s pace and interests.

Life Skills and Independence Training

Practical preparation for adulthood including functional skills and community engagement.

Diagnostic Assessments

Literacy and numeracy profiling to inform targeted intervention.

SEMH Interventions

Trauma informed strategies to support emotional regulation and resilience.

Wellbeing and PSHE Packages

Creative therapeutic sessions that promote self awareness, relationships and personal growth.

Transitional Services

Carefully planned reintegration or onward pathways supported by multi agency collaboration.

Therapeutic and Curriculum Linked Activities

Activities tailored to each learner’s interests, linked to curriculum and supported by ASDAN and AQA Awards.
