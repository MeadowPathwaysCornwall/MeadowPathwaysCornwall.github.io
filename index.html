Our mission

We support children and young people who have experienced educational trauma, anxiety, bereavement, or whose needs are not met in mainstream settings. We combine wellbeing, therapeutic practice and flexible learning so every learner can rebuild confidence, re‑engage with education and thrive in community settings.

What we do — impact

We work with young people and families to reduce anxiety, restore relationships with learning, and create sustainable pathways back into education. Outcomes include improved attendance, emotional regulation, recognised qualifications and strengthened family relationships.
