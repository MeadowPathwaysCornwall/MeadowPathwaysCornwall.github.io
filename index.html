Our Vision

Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall ethos is rooted with care and collaboration. We focus on nurturing growth, building confidence, and preparing young individuals for a brighter, more connected future. MPWEC provide bespoke therapeutic and educational support for children with SEMH and neurodiverse needs. We believe every child deserves a nurturing environment where they can grow, learn, and thrive.
