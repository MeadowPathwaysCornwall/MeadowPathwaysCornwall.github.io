At Meadow Pathways Well-being and Education, we provide bespoke, therapeutic, and educational support for children and young people who need something different. We are an EOTAS (Education Otherwise Than At School) provider based in Cornwall, offering flexible, nurturing learning experiences beyond the traditional classroom.

We support children and young people with a wide range of needs, including those who are neurodiverse, have experienced bereavement or trauma, are affected by emotionally based school non-attendance (EBSNA), or who have faced educational trauma. Our learners all have an Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP), and our work is shaped by the provisions and aspirations outlined in each plan.
