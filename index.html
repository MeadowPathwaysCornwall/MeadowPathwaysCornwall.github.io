Welcome to Meadow Pathways Cornwall

At Meadow Pathways, we offer flexible, trauma-informed support for children and young people across Cornwall. Our work is rooted in emotional safety, creativity, and connection — meeting each child where they are and walking alongside them with care and hope.

We specialise in supporting those with:

  • SEN & SEMH needs
  • Emotionally Based School Non-Attendance (EBSNA)
  • Educational trauma, bereavement & Loss, and anxiety
  • Complex journeys that don’t fit a traditional school models

Our provision is relational, therapeutic, and tailored to each child’s unique story. Whether we’re learning in the woods, baking together, exploring through play, or rebuilding confidence step by step, every moment is guided by our ethos:

  • 🛡️ Safety – creating environments where children feel secure
  • 👀 Presence – being consistently available and reliable
  • 🎧 Attunement – noticing and responding to children’s needs
  • 🤗 Connection – building trust through authentic relationships
  • 🌟 Empowerment – supporting children to find their voice and agency

We work closely with families, schools, and professionals to ensure joined-up support. Our team brings deep experience in counselling, play therapy, education, and pastoral care always led by compassion and clarity.

Meadow Pathways is more than a service, it’s a place to belong. A place where children feel seen, heard, and supported. A place where discovery, fun and learning go hand in hand.
