Welcome to Meadow Pathways Cornwall

Well-being and Education Through Individual Journeys At Meadow Pathways, we offer a nurturing, flexible alternative to traditional schooling — tailored to meet the unique needs of every child and young person. As part of Cornwall’s EOTAS provision, we work closely with families, professionals, and local community resources to create truly bespoke learning experiences. Whether it's accessing therapeutic support, outdoor education, creative arts, or vocational opportunities, each pathway is designed to help learners thrive — academically, emotionally, and socially. We believe that no two journeys are the same. That’s why we walk alongside each child, helping them rediscover their confidence, build positive relationships, and grow in a way that feels right for them.
