What we offer

Person-centred therapeutic and educational support in a nature-inspired setting. We work with schools, families and professionals to re-engage children and young people with learning and wellbeing.

Therapeutic support

Trauma-informed counselling, play therapy and family work designed to build emotional safety and resilience.

EOTAS provision

Alternative education packages, personalised learning and reintegration planning for children not attending mainstream school.
