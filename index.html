Welcome

Meadow Pathways provides inclusive therapeutic education and wellbeing support for children and young people with SEND and SEMH needs. We combine tailored learning, therapeutic practice, and community‑based activities to build confidence, skills, and a sense of belonging. 🌱

Our approach

We work relationally, using trauma informed practice and creative, curriculum‑linked activities. Our work is guided by Safety, Playfulness, Acceptance, Curiosity, and Empathy.

📅 Meeting learners where they are

We are committed to working collaboratively with families, schools, and partners to find the most meaningful and sustainable route to success for each learner. We meet every child where they are and help them move forward with confidence.

Person‑centred commitment 🌟

We create tailored routes to success matched to each learner’s needs and strengths.

Practical learning in community settings 📅

Hands‑on activities, bush craft, cooking, outdoor fitness, gardening and project work help learners build skills, resilience and positive relationships while working towards accredited outcomes. 🧠🌲🍳

Our values

Our values shape everything we do. We believe education and wellbeing go hand in hand, and every child deserves to feel safe, supported, and inspired. 🌿✨

Resilience & Independence
Health & Wellbeing
Honesty & Integrity
Friendship & Kindness

These values guide our team, our decisions, and our partnerships with families, schools, and professionals.

What we offer 🎯

We provide a fully bespoke package of care and education that includes:

Mentoring and Advocacy 🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Building trust and self‑belief through consistent relational support and alternative provisions.

Tailored Learning Packages 📚

Flexible academic pathways matched to each learner’s pace and interests.

Life Skills 🛠

Functional skills and community engagement.

Diagnostic Assessments 🧮

Literacy and numeracy profiling to inform targeted intervention.

SEMH Interventions 🧠

Trauma‑informed strategies to support emotional regulation and resilience.

Wellbeing & PSHE 🎨

Creative therapeutic sessions that promote self‑awareness, relationships and personal growth.

Transitional Services 🔄

Carefully planned reintegration or onward pathways with multi‑agency collaboration.

Therapeutic & Curriculum Activities 🧠

Nature‑based bush craft, walking, fitness, water‑based activities, fishing, climbing, cooking, play, biking and more — linked to ASDAN & AQA Unit Awards.
