Welcome to Meadow Pathways

Providing trauma-informed, person-centred education and wellbeing support across Cornwall.

Our Approach

Meadow Pathways provides tailored, nurturing environments that prioritise wellbeing, emotional regulation and holistic development. We are rooted in restorative, relational, and trauma-informed practice.

Support We Offer

We specialise in supporting those with SEND, SEMH, Emotionally Based School Non-Attendance, bereavement, and the effects of educational trauma.
