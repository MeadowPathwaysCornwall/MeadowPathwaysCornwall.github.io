Welcome to Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall

Guiding individual journeys with care and purpose. We support children and young people with SEN, SEMH, educational trauma, bereavement, and Emotionally Based School Non-Attendance (EBSNA).

Our Commitment

We provide trauma-informed, person-centred support that prioritises emotional safety, relationship-building, and practical learning. Sessions are tailored, paced, and co-produced with families, schools and partner agencies to help each child feel seen, heard and supported.

We work across community settings, including outdoor learning spaces, local halls and in-school provision when needed. Our team includes qualified counsellors, play therapists and sessional practitioners who collaborate closely with families to plan safe, achievable goals.

Provision by Age Group

Ages 5–7

Play-based intervention, building trust, simple routines, emotion regulation and early social skills in small group or 1:1 settings.

Ages 8–11

Practical life skills, therapeutic play, and small-group learning to support wellbeing, attendance and re-engagement with learning.

Ages 11–16

Trauma-informed counselling, bespoke EOTAS packages, transition planning and life-skills coaching to support resilience and independence.
