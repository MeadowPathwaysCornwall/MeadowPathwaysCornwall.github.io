Welcome to Meadow Pathways Cornwall

Guiding individual journeys with care and purpose

Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall offers flexible, trauma-informed support for children and young people navigating emotionally based school non-attendance, educational trauma, bereavement, and SEN/SEMH needs.

We work relationally, creatively, and collaboratively to build trust and tailor every journey to the child’s unique needs. Our team includes qualified therapists, educators, and mentors who deliver therapeutic curriculum-linked activities, life skills, and emotional support in safe, nurturing environments — from woodland walks to beach sessions, bushcraft, and beyond.

We welcome referrals from families, schools, and professionals. Explore our site to learn more about our ethos, services, and how we can support your child’s journey.
