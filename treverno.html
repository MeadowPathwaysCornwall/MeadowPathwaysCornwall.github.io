Welcome to Treverno Meadows

Treverno Meadows provides a nurturing, trauma-informed environment designed to support children and young people who may struggle to engage in mainstream education. Our approach combines therapeutic practice, creative learning, and nature-based experiences to promote wellbeing and personal growth.

The Treverno provision is part of Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall, offering bespoke, person-centred support to meet individual needs. We work closely with families, schools, and local authorities to ensure that every young person has the opportunity to re-engage with learning in a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment.
