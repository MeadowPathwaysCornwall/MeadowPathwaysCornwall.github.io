Our Approach

At Treverno, we provide a blend of therapeutic practice, curriculum-linked activities, and outdoor experiential learning to promote engagement, emotional regulation, and academic progress.

Provision Highlights

  • Small group and 1:1 teaching to meet individual learning goals
  • Curriculum-linked creative activities, including arts, cooking, and bushcraft
  • Therapeutic interventions to support SEMH needs
  • Life skills development to prepare for independence
  • Multi-agency collaboration with families, schools, and external partners
  • Assessment and monitoring of progress with bespoke reports

Learning Environment

Treverno Meadows provides a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment. Outdoor learning spaces and community-based projects encourage confidence, resilience, and positive relationships.
