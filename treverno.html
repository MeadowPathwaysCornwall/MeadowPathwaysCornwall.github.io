Treverno Meadows

Welcome to Treverno Meadows, our new site for Meadow Pathways Wellbeing & Education Cornwall. This centre is designed to provide children and young people with a safe, nurturing environment where they can reconnect with learning and life.

Our Vision

Treverno Meadows is a trauma-informed learning hub integrating therapeutic support with bespoke education packages. Our focus is on re-engaging young people, fostering confidence, and promoting meaningful educational achievements.

Facilities & Activities

  • Bespoke classroom spaces for one-to-one or small group learning
  • Outdoor activity zones for bushcraft, gardening, and fitness
  • Creative arts spaces for painting, multimedia, and music
  • Cooking and life skills kitchens
  • Therapeutic areas for counselling, speech and language therapy, and wellbeing sessions

Who We Support

Treverno Meadows is open to children and young people who require alternative provision due to SEMH needs, SEND, school non-attendance, or other barriers to mainstream education.

Our Approach

We work relationally, blending curriculum-linked activities with therapeutic support to ensure each learner achieves personal and educational growth. Sessions are tailored to individual needs and interests, with qualifications and recognition through ASDAN and AQA Unit Awards.
