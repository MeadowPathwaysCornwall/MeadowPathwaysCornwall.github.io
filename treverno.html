Our Approach

At Treverno, we work relationally, recognising each learner's unique strengths and needs. We integrate therapeutic practices with curriculum-linked activities to build confidence, resilience, and engagement.

What We Offer

  • Mentoring and Advocacy: Building trust and self-belief through consistent relational support.
  • Tailored Learning Packages: Flexible academic pathways tailored to individual pace and interests.
  • Life Skills & Independence Development: Practical preparation for adulthood including community engagement.
  • Diagnostic Assessments: Literacy and numeracy profiling to inform targeted support.
  • SEMH Interventions: Trauma-informed strategies to support emotional regulation and resilience.
  • Wellbeing & PSHE: Creative therapeutic sessions promoting self-awareness, relationships, and growth.
  • Transitional Services: Planned reintegration or onward pathways supported by multi-agency collaboration.
  • Therapeutic & Curriculum Activities: Outdoor activities, bush craft, cooking, water-based activities, climbing, play, and more, linked to ASDAN & AQA Unit Awards.

Who We Support

Children and young people unable to access mainstream education due to SEMH needs, SEND, or barriers to learning. Our bespoke provision is flexible and responsive to individual interests and strengths.
