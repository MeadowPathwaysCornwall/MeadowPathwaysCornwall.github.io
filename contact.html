About Us

Meadow Pathways Cornwall is dedicated to providing inclusive, nurturing, and bespoke education for all young people. Meadow Pathways Well-being and Education is a developing, independent EOTAS (Education Otherwise Than At School) provision designed to support children and young people with Social, Emotional and Mental Health (SEMH) needs and a wide range of neurodiversity.
