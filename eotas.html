EOTAS Provision

Meadow Pathways provides bespoke EOTAS (Education Other Than At School) packages for children and young people who are unable to access mainstream education due to SEMH needs, trauma, or other barriers. Our approach is relational, creative, and trauma-informed, designed to meet each learner where they are and support them to move forward with confidence.

What we offer

  • Tailored learning – ASDAN, AQA Unit Awards, Functional Skills, and curriculum-linked activities matched to each learner’s pace and interests.
  • Therapeutic support – Play therapy, emotion coaching, and SEMH interventions delivered by qualified practitioners.
  • Community-based sessions – Nature walks, bushcraft, cooking, and practical life skills to build independence and resilience.
  • Mentoring and advocacy – Consistent relational support to build trust, self-belief, and a sense of belonging.
  • Diagnostic assessments – Literacy and numeracy profiling to inform targeted intervention and planning.

Our approach

We work flexibly across Cornwall, supporting children in their homes, communities, and dedicated learning spaces. Our team builds strong relationships with families and professionals to ensure every child’s journey is supported with care and purpose.

Referrals

We accept referrals from schools, local authorities, parents, and professionals. To make a referral, please visit our Referral page.
