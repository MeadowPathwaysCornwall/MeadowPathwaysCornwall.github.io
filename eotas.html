EOTAS and Alternative Education

Personalised education packages to support children and young people who are out of mainstream school or experiencing anxiety-based non-attendance.

What EOTAS offers

We deliver tailored learning plans, therapeutic interventions and re-engagement work that supports progress, wellbeing and a safe return to education when appropriate.

Individual learning plans

Plans are co-produced with families and schools, with clear targets, progress tracking and regular reviews.

Accreditation and pathways

Where appropriate we support accredited learning and transition planning into mainstream school or college.

How to refer to EOTAS

Use the referral form or contact us directly for an initial discussion. If the referral involves safeguarding concerns, contact us by phone first.
