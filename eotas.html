EOTAS at Meadow Pathways

Bespoke Education Otherwise Than At School provision, integrating therapeutic support with personalised education.

SEND and EOTAS

Provision can be agreed through EHCP processes and tailored to meet the needs set out in Section F of an EHCP.

What is EOTAS?

Education Otherwise Than At School is provision for children and young people where suitable education cannot be delivered in a mainstream setting. It must be agreed with the local authority as part of an EHC needs assessment or review.

Our EOTAS offer

Meadow Pathways provides bespoke EOTAS packages across Cornwall. We combine therapeutic input with curriculum-linked activities, small group and one-to-one delivery, and accredited outcomes through ASDAN and AQA Unit Awards.

Who we support

We support learners with SEND, SEMH, school avoidance, or complex needs that require alternative provision to promote re-engagement with learning and wellbeing.

How to explore EOTAS

If you would like to discuss EOTAS for a child or young person, please contact the Meadow Pathways team or complete a referral form. For urgent safeguarding concerns, contact statutory services first.
