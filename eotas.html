What is EOTAS?

EOTAS stands for Education Otherwise Than At School. It is a specialised educational provision arranged by the Local Authority for children and young people with an Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) whose needs cannot be met within a traditional school environment.

EOTAS packages can include up to 25 hours per week during term time. Examples include one-to-one tuition, online or home-based learning, life skills development, mentoring, and therapeutic support.

At Meadow Pathways, we believe in personalised learning journeys guided with care, patience, and understanding.
