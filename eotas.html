Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS)

We provide EOTAS provision for learners who require an alternative to mainstream schooling. Our programmes are bespoke and designed to meet individual educational and wellbeing needs.

Referral and assessment

Referrals can be made by local authorities, schools, or professionals working with young people. We complete a careful assessment and create a personalised learning plan in partnership with families and agencies.

Curriculum & accreditation

We offer tailored curricula that can include accreditation where appropriate. Please see our Curriculum Policy (EOTAS) and contact us for further details.
