Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS)

Meadow Pathways Cornwall specialises in providing bespoke Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS) packages for children and young people who are unable to access mainstream education due to a variety of reasons, including neurodivergence, trauma, emotional-based school non-attendance (EBSNA), bereavement, and more.

Our EOTAS provision is designed to be flexible and tailored to each learner’s unique needs, focusing on building confidence, resilience, and a love for learning in a safe, supportive environment.

We work closely with local authorities, families, and a range of specialist support services to develop and implement personalised education plans that address not only academic needs but also emotional and social well-being.

Our approach includes therapeutic education on-site, outreach learning within the community, and creative programmes that engage learners in meaningful and relevant ways.

We understand the importance of collaboration, and our team regularly liaises with educational psychologists, social workers, and other professionals to ensure multi-agency support is coordinated effectively.

For more information or to discuss how our EOTAS services can support your child or young person, please contact us via the Contact Us page.
