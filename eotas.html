Education Other Than At School (EOTAS)

Tailored learning and wellbeing support for children and young people

About EOTAS Provision

Meadow Pathways provides bespoke EOTAS support for children and young people who are unable to access mainstream education due to social, emotional, or mental health needs. Our approach is trauma-informed, person-centred, and focused on reintegration wherever possible.

We collaborate closely with local authorities, schools, and families to develop individualised education plans that include wellbeing, academic progress, and social development. Our EOTAS provision encompasses both short-term and long-term placements to meet a variety of needs.

Key Benefits of Our EOTAS Provision

  • Personalised learning tailored to each young person's needs
  • Safe and supportive environment
  • Trauma-informed teaching strategies
  • Integration of wellbeing and creative therapies
  • Close collaboration with families and local authorities
  • Focus on re-engagement with learning and positive outcomes
