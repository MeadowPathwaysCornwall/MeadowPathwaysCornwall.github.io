Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS)

Meadow Pathways Cornwall specialises in Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS) packages tailored to meet the individual needs of children and young people unable to attend mainstream education.

Our bespoke packages include on-site therapeutic education, outreach support, and community-based learning. We aim to foster a supportive, trauma-informed environment that nurtures academic progress alongside emotional and social well-being.

Key Features of Our EOTAS Provision

  • Individualised learning plans designed to suit each learner’s strengths and challenges
  • Multi-disciplinary approach with collaboration from families, schools, and specialist services
  • Flexibility to adapt programmes to changing needs
  • Holistic support addressing mental health, wellbeing, and social skills
  • Qualified and trauma-informed staff delivering expert education and care

For more information or to discuss how our EOTAS provision might support your child, please contact us.
