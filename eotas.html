What is EOTAS?

EOTAS stands for Education Otherwise Than At School. It is a specialised educational provision arranged by the Local Authority for children and young people with an Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) whose needs cannot be met within a traditional school environment.

At Meadow Pathways Well-being and Education, we offer EOTAS packages that are thoughtful, creative, and tailored to the individual. Our provision supports children across Cornwall, creating personalised paths to learning, well-being, and growth.

What might an EOTAS package include?

EOTAS packages can include up to 25 hours per week during term time. Examples include:

  • One-to-one tuition
  • Online or home-based learning
  • Life skills development
  • Mentoring and therapeutic support
  • Creative or vocational education
