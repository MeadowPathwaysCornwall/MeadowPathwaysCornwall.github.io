Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS)

Guiding Individual Journeys with Care and Purpose

What is EOTAS?

Education Otherwise Than At School is special educational provision delivered outside a formal school setting. It can only be agreed with the local authority through EHC assessment or EHCP review processes. Provision may take place at home or in other non-registered settings, depending on the child’s needs and agreed plan.

  • Online or virtual learning
  • Home tuition
  • Tuition centres or specialist learning hubs
  • Hospital schooling
  • Therapeutic input (SaLT, OT, Physio, Counselling, Play Therapy)

Provision should be detailed in Section F of the EHCP, which the local authority must secure and fund.

Our EOTAS offer

Meadow Pathways provides bespoke EOTAS provision across Cornwall. Wellbeing sits at the heart of learning, integrating therapeutic support with personalised education. We work closely with families and partner agencies to plan for emotional safety, confidence-building, and purposeful learning.

How we work

We begin with listening, then use diagnostic assessments and profiling to design targeted learning. Sessions blend therapeutic practice with curriculum-linked activities so learning feels meaningful and transferable.
