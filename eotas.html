EOTAS Provision

What we offer

Our Education Other Than At School (EOTAS) provision provides bespoke learning packages for children and young people unable to attend mainstream school due to social, emotional and mental health needs, educational trauma or other barriers to attendance.

Core elements

  • Individual learning plans with clear targets
  • Small group and 1:1 teaching
  • Therapeutic curriculum elements
  • Hands-on practical activities to build engagement

Referral routes

Referrals may be made by schools, parents or multi-agency partners. Please use the Referral form to begin the process.
