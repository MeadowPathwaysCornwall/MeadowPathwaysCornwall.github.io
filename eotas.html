Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall

Meadow Pathways offers a bespoke Education Other Than At School (EOTAS) provision for children and young people across Cornwall who may find mainstream education challenging. Our focus is on providing a therapeutic, nature-based, and alternative learning experience that supports both academic growth and personal development.

Set in the natural surroundings and out in the community of Cornwall, Meadow Pathways blends outdoor learning, wellbeing activities, and personalised teaching to help each learner rebuild confidence, develop independence, and re-engage with education at their own pace.
