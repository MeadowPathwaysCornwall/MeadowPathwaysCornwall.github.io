What is EOTAS?

EOTAS stands for Education Otherwise Than At School. It is a specialised educational provision arranged by the Local Authority for children and young people with an Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) whose needs cannot be met within a traditional school environment.

EOTAS packages can include up to 25 hours per week during term time. Examples include:

  • One-to-one tuition
  • Online or home-based learning
  • Life skills development
  • Mentoring and therapeutic support
  • Creative or vocational education

At Meadow Pathways, we believe in personalised learning journeys guided with care, patience, and understanding. Our approach focuses on empowering children and young people to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally, tailoring the learning experience to their unique needs and aspirations.

Our EOTAS provision emphasizes:

  • Building confidence and resilience
  • Encouraging creativity and independent thinking
  • Providing therapeutic interventions where needed
  • Creating a supportive and safe learning environment
  • Fostering positive relationships between educators, learners, and families

We work closely with families, local authorities, and other professionals to design and deliver bespoke EOTAS packages that meet statutory requirements and, most importantly, the needs of the child or young person. Every individual deserves the opportunity to succeed, and we are here to help them find their path.
