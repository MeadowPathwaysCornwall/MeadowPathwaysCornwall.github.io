EOTAS Provision

Our EOTAS packages are bespoke, child-centred programmes combining therapeutic support and flexible learning. We create tailored timetables, practical learning opportunities and relational support to help children re-engage with education.

Typical elements

  • One-to-one therapeutic education
  • Small group sessions for social skills
  • Outdoor learning and practical skills
  • Family and school liaison

If you’d like to discuss an EOTAS package, please refer a child or get in touch.
