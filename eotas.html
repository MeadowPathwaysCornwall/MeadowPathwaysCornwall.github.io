Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS)

Bespoke, wellbeing-led provision where school is not appropriate.

What is EOTAS?

Education Otherwise Than At School is the delivery of special educational provision outside a formal school setting, agreed with the local authority within the EHCP process when needs cannot be met in school.

Our EOTAS offer

We provide bespoke EOTAS provision across Cornwall, placing wellbeing at the heart of learning, integrating therapeutic support with personalised education. We work closely with families and partner agencies to plan purposeful learning that supports emotional safety and re-engages young people.

Who we support

Children/young people unable to attend mainstream due to SEMH, SEND, school avoidance, or where tailored approaches are needed to re-engage with education.

How we work

We listen first. We plan bespoke packages that blend therapeutic approaches with curriculum-linked activities. Sessions include one-to-one, small group, and community-based learning to build skills, confidence, and positive connections.

What we offer

  • Mentoring & Advocacy
  • Tailored learning packages
  • Life Skills & Independence
  • Diagnostic Assessments
  • SEMH interventions
  • Wellbeing & PSHE packages
  • Transitional services
  • Nature-based activities linked to ASDAN/AQA
