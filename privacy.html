Privacy Notice

How Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall handles personal data

Who we are

Meadow Pathways Wellbeing and Education Cornwall Ltd provides inclusive therapeutic education and wellbeing support for children and young people across Cornwall.

What personal data we collect

  • Contact details you provide via referral or contact forms (name, email, phone).
  • Information needed to deliver services (dates, session notes, basic educational details) where supplied by schools, parents or carers.
  • Form responses submitted via our online forms (referral, hours logging).

Why we collect it and lawful basis

We collect and process personal data to deliver educational and wellbeing services, to respond to enquiries and referrals, and to meet our safeguarding and regulatory duties. Our lawful bases include consent, contractual necessity, and compliance with legal obligations where required.

How we store and protect data

We store personal data securely. Sensitive or internal documents intended only for staff are retained on our authenticated SharePoint and not published publicly on the website. Public files stored in the repository are accessible to anyone and should not contain personal data.

Who we share data with

We share personal data only with third parties where necessary to deliver services (for example, partner agencies, statutory services, or when required by law). We do not sell personal data to third parties.

How long we keep data

Retention periods vary by record type and legal requirements. If you need specific retention information for a particular record, contact us using the details below.

Your rights

  • Right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Right to request erasure, restriction, or portability where applicable.
  • Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
